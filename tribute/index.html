Here is the timeline of Mary Queen of Scots
  • 1542: Mary was born, and at just 6 days old was crowned Queen after the passing of her father James V
  • 1548: Mary was sent to France at 5 years old
  • 1558: Just 10 years later, Mary marries the dophane of France, Francis II
  • 1559: Francis becomes king of France after the tragic death of his father, making Mary the Queen Consort of France
  • 1560: King Francis traically looses his life, and Mary Returns to her home Scotland where her reign was struggling
  • 1565: After 5 years, Mary then marries Lord Darnley in attempt for succession in Scotland
  • 1566: Mary then bared an heir to Scotland, James
  • 1567: Mary's husband, Darnley was murdered, and marries Earl of Bothwell
  • 1567: just the same year, Mary was force to abdicate the throne by nobles who apposed the current marriage
  • 1568: Mary seeks refuge by Queen Elizabeth I, who was also her cousin and rival
  • 1587: Sadly, Mary was executed for treason after 19 years of imprisonment
